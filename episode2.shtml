StyleThis episode's writing style, as identified by Stuart Gilbert in his scheme of Ulysses (as cited on Moxham, http://www.ulysses-art.demon.co.uk/scheme.html), is "Narrative: Young". The narrative style is probably the style you are most used to with storytelling; notable here is the "young" morning style, as delivered by the young Stephen Dedalus, as opposed to the "mature" narrative style used during the description of Bloom's adult habits in the "Calypso" episode, and the "old" narrative style used in the "Eumaeus" episode at the low point of the day.
